Origami Fish - Simple & Fun

Make a cute paper fish in just a few folds! Perfect for kids and beginners.

Difficulty: Easy Time: 2 minutes

Materials Needed

  • 1 square piece of paper (any color)

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Fold Diagonally

Fold the square paper in half diagonally to form a triangle.

2

Fold Corners

Fold the bottom left and right corners up to meet at the top point.

3

Create Fish Shape

Fold the side edges to the center, leaving a small triangle at the top for the tail. Flip over and your fish is done!
