Jumping Frog Origami - Fun Paper Toy!

Make a paper frog that actually jumps! Press its back and watch it hop across the table.

Difficulty: Medium Time: 5 minutes It actually jumps!

Materials Needed

  • 1 rectangular piece of paper

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Start with Rectangle

Fold top right corner to left edge, then unfold. Repeat with left corner to right edge.

2

Form Triangle

Using the X crease as a guide, push the sides in to form a triangle.

3

Fold Legs

Fold both bottom corners upward at an angle. Fold the bottom edge up, then fold back to create the spring.

4

Complete Frog

Fold the top part down to form the head. Turn over and press the back to make it jump!
