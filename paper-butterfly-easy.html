Easy Paper Butterfly - Beautiful & Simple

Create a lovely paper butterfly in minutes! Great for decorations, party favors, or as a gift.

Difficulty: Easy Time: 3 minutes

Materials Needed

  • 1 square piece of paper
  • Pipe cleaner (optional)

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Fold Square

Fold the square paper in half diagonally, then unfold.

2

Make Accordion Folds

Fold the edges to meet the center crease, then fold in half like an accordion.

3

Form Butterfly

Pinch the center to form the body. Pull the wings out and adjust. Add antenna if desired!
