Origami Heart - Simple & Cute

Fold a sweet origami heart in just a few minutes! A perfect craft for Valentine's Day or to show someone you love them.

Difficulty: Easy Time: 3 minutes Perfect for gifts

Materials Needed

  • 1 square piece of paper (red or pink)

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Fold Diagonally

Place paper colored-side down. Fold diagonally both ways to create an X crease.

2

Form Triangle

Fold in half horizontally and vertically to create a + crease. Push sides in to form a small square.

3

Make Triangle

Fold the bottom point up to the top edge to form a triangle.

4

Shape Heart

Fold both bottom corners up at an angle. Flip over and fold corners inward to form the heart shape!
