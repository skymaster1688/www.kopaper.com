Paper Hat Craft - Fun for Kids!

Make a simple paper hat in just 2 minutes! Perfect for kids to wear or use as a prop.

Difficulty: Very Easy Time: 2 minutes

Materials Needed

  • 1 sheet of newspaper or large paper

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Fold in Half

Fold the bottom edge up to meet the top edge.

2

Fold Corners

Fold both top corners down to the center, overlapping slightly.

3

Complete Hat

Fold the bottom edges up on both sides. Turn over and your hat is ready!
