Origami Crane - Traditional Japanese Art

The origami crane is a symbol of peace and good fortune. Learn how to fold this beautiful traditional Japanese craft with our easy tutorial!

Difficulty: Medium Time: 10 minutes Symbol: Peace

Materials Needed

  • 1 square piece of origami paper (15cm x 15cm)
  • Flat work surface

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Create Square Base

Start with colored side down. Fold diagonally both ways, then unfold. Fold in half horizontally and vertically to create an X crease pattern.

2

Form Double Triangle

Push the sides inward while bringing the bottom and top together. You now have a double triangle (square base).

3

Petal Fold

Fold the left and right edges to the center line. Lift the bottom point up while pushing the sides inward. Repeat on the other side.

4

Form Bird Base

Fold the legs inward at an angle. Fold the top triangle down on both sides. Repeat the petal fold.

5

Create Head and Tail

Make a small fold for the head. Fold the tail section in half vertically (inside reverse fold). Open the wings gently!

Pro Tips

  • Use slightly larger paper for beginners
  • Crease sharply at each step
  • Traditional origami paper has different colors on each side
