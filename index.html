FAQ

Questions? We've Got Answers

Is this Paper to PPT tool really free?

Yes! koPaper's Paper to PPT converter is free to use. No signup required, no watermarks on your downloaded presentations.

How does the Paper to PPT conversion work?

Paste your text, paper, or article into the text box. Each paragraph becomes an individual slide. The first line of each paragraph becomes the slide title, and the rest becomes bullet points. Choose your style and click generate to download a real .pptx file.

Can I edit the PPT after downloading?

Absolutely! The downloaded .pptx file is a fully standard PowerPoint file. Open in Microsoft PowerPoint, Google Slides, Apple Keynote, or WPS Office and edit everything — text, colors, images, layouts, and more.

Is my content stored or sent to a server?

No. Your content stays private. The entire PPT generation process happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your text is never uploaded to any server or stored in any database.

What presentation styles are available?

We offer 5 styles: Business (blue theme), Clean & Minimal (white with accents), Creative (gradient design), Academic (formal layout), and Simple Outline (text-focused). Each has unique colors and typography.

Can I convert PDFs or Word documents?

Currently, paste your text directly into the text box. For PDFs and Word documents, simply copy the text from your document and paste it here. We're working on direct file upload support.
