Simple Paper Fox - Cute & Easy!

Fold an adorable fox face in just a few minutes! Perfect for beginners and kids.

Difficulty: Easy Time: 3 minutes

Materials Needed

  • 1 square piece of orange paper
  • White and black paper for details

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Fold Diagonally

Fold the square paper in half diagonally to form a triangle.

2

Form Ears

Fold both bottom corners up at an angle to create the fox ears.

3

Complete Face

Fold the bottom point up slightly. Add white circles for eyes and a black nose. Your fox is complete!
