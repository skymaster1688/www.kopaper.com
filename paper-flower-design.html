Paper Flower Design - Beautiful DIY Craft

Learn how to create stunning paper flowers that look real! Perfect for home decoration, gifts, or party decor.

Difficulty: Easy-Medium Time: 15 minutes Great for decor

Materials Needed

  • Colored paper (multiple colors)
  • Scissors
  • Glue or tape
  • Wooden sticks (optional)

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Cut Petals

Cut 5-6 petal shapes from colored paper. Make them oval or heart-shaped.

2

Shape Petals

curl the petals slightly using a pencil or your fingers to give them depth.

3

Assemble Flower

Layer the petals, starting with the largest at back. Glue each petal overlapping the previous one.

4

Add Center

Roll a small piece of paper into a circle for the flower center. Glue in place.

Pro Tips

  • Use tissue paper for delicate flowers
  • Mix colors for variety
  • Add leaves from green paper
