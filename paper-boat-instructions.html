How to Make a Paper Boat

Make a classic paper boat that floats! This easy tutorial shows you how to create a boat that can actually sail on water.

Difficulty: Easy Time: 3 minutes Floats on water!

Materials Needed

  • 1 sheet of paper (A4 or Letter)

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Start with Rectangle

Place paper in landscape orientation. Fold top corner down to bottom edge, crease, and unfold.

2

Form Triangle

Fold the bottom half up, aligning with the diagonal crease. You now have a triangle with extra paper at bottom.

3

Complete Triangle

Fold the extra bottom portions in on each side, then fold the bottom point up to create a smaller triangle.

4

Open the Boat

Hold the triangle point down. Pull apart the sides and push the bottom up to form a diamond. Fold down the top points and pull apart to complete your boat!

Pro Tips

  • Use wax paper for a waterproof boat
  • Add small paper sails for decoration
  • Test in a tub or sink first
