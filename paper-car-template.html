Paper Car Template - Build Your Own!

Download and print a paper car template. Cut, fold, and glue to create an awesome paper toy car!

Difficulty: Medium Time: 20 minutes Fun toy!

What You Need

  • Printed car template
  • Scissors
  • Glue stick
  • Cardstock paper (optional)

Instructions

1

Print Template

Generate and print the car template using our AI tool.

2

Cut Out Parts

Carefully cut out all the car parts along the solid lines.

3

Score Fold Lines

Use a ruler and ballpoint pen to score along the dotted fold lines.

4

Assemble

Fold and glue the parts together. Start with the body, then add wheels and details!
