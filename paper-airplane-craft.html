How to Make a Paper Airplane - Easy Tutorial

Learn how to make a classic paper airplane that flies smoothly with this simple step-by-step guide. Perfect for kids and beginners!

Difficulty: Easy Time: 5 minutes Age: 6+

Materials Needed

  • 1 sheet of paper (A4 or Letter size)
  • Flat surface to work on

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Prepare the Paper

Start with a rectangular piece of paper. Place it in landscape orientation (horizontal).

2

First Fold

Fold the paper in half lengthwise. Crease well, then unfold. This creates a center line guide.

3

Fold Corners

Fold the top right corner down to the center line. Repeat with the top left corner.

4

Form the Nose

Fold the angled edges to the center line again, making a narrower shape.

5

Create the Wings

Fold the plane in half along the center. Then fold down the wings on both sides.

Pro Tips

  • Use thinner paper for better flight
  • Fold sharply for straighter flight
  • Adjust the wing angle to change flight direction
